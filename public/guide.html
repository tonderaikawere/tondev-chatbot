User Guide

Getting Started

Open the app and select a mentor based on your area of interest: frontend, backend, security, Python, mobile, or cloud.

Asking Questions

Type your question in the chat box. The AI mentor will answer using its knowledge base. Try to be specific for best results.

Choosing Mentors

Each mentor specializes in a different tech field. Switch mentors anytime from the sidebar to get the best answer.

Troubleshooting

  • If you get a fallback answer, try rephrasing your question.
  • Make sure you are chatting with the right mentor for your topic.
  • If the app is blank, refresh or restart it.
